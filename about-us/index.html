A fresh perspective...

We are a team of experienced HR and Business Development professionals with expertise in a diverse range of industry sectors.

The team is led by Julie Kellett and Sarah Birkby.

Julie Kellett

A member of the Chartered Institute of Personnel and Development, Julie has over 20 years of Human Resource experience gained within the Hospitality and Retail sectors.

Past companies include Holiday Inn, a London Private members Club and more recently, Blockbuster Entertainment, where she been promoted through several HR Management roles to become Vice President of Human Resources for Blockbuster UK.

Throughout her career Julie has supported businesses with: employee relations, performance management, resource and talent planning, merger activities plus organisational and leadership development.

She has a particular specialism in Occupational testing and holds a number of related qualifications including being British Psychological Society Level A eligible; British Psychological Level B intermediate eligible as well as being an MBTI Step One & Two practitioner.

She also holds a postgraduate diploma from the University of Westminster along with a HCIMA (part B) professional qualification from Leeds Polytechnic.

Sarah Birkby

Sarah has worked for over 15 years in publishing, events, marketing and business development in a wide range of roles and sectors, including publishing, research, automotive, charitable and architecture/construction.

Her corporate experience includes Informa Group plc; Mazda Motors UK; Jupiter MMXI; Woodside Communications (a division of Huntsworth plc, now Grayling) ; SHINE Trust and ADP LLP, where she is currently employed as Business Development Director.
