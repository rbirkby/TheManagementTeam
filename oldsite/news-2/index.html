Your HR Resources

We regularly post our thoughts on how to deal with the common ‘people problems’ that businesses of all shapes and sizes, face. You’ll find these posted to our Worry Wall below. From time to time, we’ll also publicise consultancy offers giving you access to specialist HR advice on a first-come, first-served basis.

We welcome your queries and do get in touch if you think we can help more.

New HR Clinic for Bucks Business First

 

We’re hosting a series of HR Clinics at the Bucks Business First (BBF) offices in Saunderton, providing a drop-in/quick reference service for BBF Members.

The first clinics will take place on Thursday 27th March and Thursday 10th April from 11am – 1pm.   To book an appointment contact us at: info@management-team.co.uk and check out our Member Offer at Bucks Business First.
